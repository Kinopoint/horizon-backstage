Photo & video usage

Simple guidance for downloading, posting, crediting and requesting changes.

The short version

Personal sharing is welcome.

Permitted personal use

You may download Horizon Backstage photographs and videos for personal, non-commercial use. This includes posting an image of yourself or a festival memory on a personal social-media account.

Credit

When practical, credit Horizon Festival and tag @horizonfestivalballyb. Do not remove an embedded credit or falsely claim authorship.

Uses that require permission

Advertising, resale, merchandise, editorial syndication, sponsorship campaigns, paid promotion and other commercial uses require prior written permission from the rights holder. Request permission through the official contact form.

Respectful use

Do not use the media in a deceptive, defamatory, discriminatory or harmful context. Do not use a person’s likeness to imply an endorsement they did not give.

Removal and correction requests

If you appear in an image and would like it reviewed, corrected or removed, provide the gallery title or a screenshot through the Horizon Festival contact page. The festival team can identify the relevant file from that information.

Festival recording notice

The official Horizon Festival terms and conditions include the event’s photography, filming and media notice.
