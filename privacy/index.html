About this gallery

Horizon Backstage is a static media archive for Horizon Festival Ballybunion 2026. You can browse and share media without creating an account or giving the gallery personal information.

Popularity counter

The share-click popularity counter is not active in this release. When it is enabled, this notice will identify its provider, duplicate-prevention method and retention period before collection begins.

Analytics and local storage

This release does not install advertising pixels, marketing cookies or a popularity-counter token. Interaction events remain in the page’s temporary in-memory data layer and are not transmitted to an analytics provider.

Hosting information

The gallery is hosted through GitHub Pages. Like most hosting providers, GitHub may process technical request information needed to deliver and secure the service. See the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

External websites

Links to Horizon Festival, Instagram, Facebook and other external services are governed by those services’ own privacy practices.

People shown in photographs

The festival terms explain that the event may be photographed, filmed and recorded. If you appear in the archive and want an image reviewed or removed, send the image title or a screenshot through the official Horizon Festival contact form.

Future analytics changes

If analytics or other non-essential technologies are introduced, this notice and the site’s consent controls must be updated before those tools are enabled.
